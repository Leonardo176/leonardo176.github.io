## Why am I starting a blog?

I want a place where I can talk to people freely about what I think.

### Why not create an account on social media

I could just create a social media account, and I would get a lot more visibility,
the problem is that Meta's social media platforms have a lot of problems from my
perspective.

For example, with the introduction of short form of content, people
are less incentived of content that requires time to watch/read and understand
(like a blog!).

But I will stop here as the social media topic will be for another blog.
So stay tuned!
